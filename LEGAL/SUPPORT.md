# NYX STUDIOS – SUPPORT POLICY
Last Updated: 2025

---

## 1. HOW TO REQUEST SUPPORT

For support, contact:

- **Email:** nyxstudiosinc@gmail.com

Please include when possible:

- Software name and version  
- Description of the issue  
- Steps to reproduce  
- Screenshots or logs (if available)  

---

## 2. WHAT WE SUPPORT

We provide support for:

- Installation problems  
- Crashes or critical bugs  
- Licensing or activation errors  
- Update failures  
- Basic configuration questions  

---

## 3. WHAT WE DO NOT SUPPORT

We **do not** provide support for:

- Modified, cracked, or tampered copies of the Software  
- Unauthorized redistribution or third-party builds  
- Extremely outdated versions that are no longer maintained  
- Third-party hacks, tools, or external modifications  
- Operating systems or environments outside our documented requirements  

---

## 4. RESPONSE TIME

We aim to respond to most inquiries within **48–72 hours**, excluding
weekends and holidays.

Response times may vary based on complexity and workload.

---

## 5. TERMINATION OF SUPPORT

NYX Studios may decline or terminate support for:

- Users violating license terms  
- Users engaged in abusive or hostile communication  
- Users running unauthorized or illegal copies of the Software  

**END OF SUPPORT POLICY**

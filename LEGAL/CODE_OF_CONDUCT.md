## 1. EXPECTED BEHAVIOR

Users, contributors, and community members should:

- Treat others with respect and professionalism  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Use appropriate language and tone  
- Follow repository and project guidelines  
- Act in good faith and avoid malicious behavior  

---

## 2. PROHIBITED BEHAVIOR

The following behavior is **not tolerated**:

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory remarks  
- Threats, stalking, or intimidation  
- Posting private, confidential, or copyrighted data without permission  
- Illegal activity of any kind  
- Attempts to hack, disrupt, or abuse NYX Studios services or communities  

---

## 3. ENFORCEMENT

NYX Studios may:

- Remove comments, issues, or contributions  
- Ban users from repositories or communities  
- Report abusive accounts to hosting platforms  
- Terminate access to Software or Services  

Enforcement decisions are at the sole discretion of NYX Studios.

---

## 4. REPORTING ISSUES

To report misconduct or violations of this Code of Conduct:

- **Email:** nyxstudiosinc@gmail.com

Provide as much detail as possible so we can investigate appropriately.

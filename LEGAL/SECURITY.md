NYX Studios takes security seriously. This Security Policy outlines how
we handle vulnerabilities, disclosures, and security expectations.

---

## 1. REPORTING VULNERABILITIES

If you discover a potential security issue, contact us at:

- **Email:** nyxstudiosinc@gmail.com

Please include, where possible:

- A clear description of the issue  
- Steps to reproduce  
- Potential impact  
- Proof-of-concept (if safe to share)  

---

## 2. RESPONSIBLE DISCLOSURE

By reporting a vulnerability, you agree **NOT** to:

- Publicly disclose details before we have had a reasonable time to fix it  
- Exploit the vulnerability for personal gain  
- Access, modify, or delete data that does not belong to you  
- Disrupt or degrade NYX Studios systems or services  

---

## 3. OUR COMMITMENT

NYX Studios will:

- Acknowledge receipt of your report  
- Investigate legitimate security issues  
- Work to resolve confirmed problems  
- Optionally credit researchers (if requested and appropriate)  

---

## 4. SCOPE

This policy applies to:

- NYX Studios desktop applications  
- Licensing and update servers  
- APIs and online services directly operated by NYX Studios  
- Official repositories and distributions  

---

## 5. OUT OF SCOPE

The following are **not** considered security vulnerabilities:

- Issues caused by outdated operating systems or drivers  
- Problems arising from user misconfiguration  
- Social engineering, phishing, or attacks against individual users  
- Physical attacks or theft of hardware  

---

## 6. LEGAL SAFE HARBOR

If you follow this policy and act in good faith, NYX Studios will not
initiate legal action solely for your security research and responsible
disclosure.

**END OF SECURITY POLICY**

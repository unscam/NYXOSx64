NYX Studios respects intellectual property laws and expects users to do

## 1. DMCA COMPLIANCE

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used improperly in any
NYX Studios software or repository, send a DMCA notice to:

- **Email:** nyxstudiosinc@gmail.com

Your notice **must** include:

- Your full legal name  
- Contact information  
- Description of the copyrighted work  
- Exact location (URL, file path, or repository link) of the infringing material  
- A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized  
- A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate  
- Your physical or digital signature  

---

## 2. NYX STUDIOS COPYRIGHT PROTECTION

We will file takedown requests and enforce our rights against:

- Unauthorized distribution or reupload of our software  
- Cracks, keygens, reverse-engineered builds, or modified copies  
- Use of NYX Studios branding, trademarks, or assets without permission  
- Derivative works that infringe our copyrights  

---

## 3. COUNTER-NOTIFICATIONS

If you believe a takedown was issued in error, you may submit a
counter-notice including:

- Your full legal name and contact information  
- Identification of the removed material and its previous location  
- A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you have a good-faith belief
  the removal was a mistake or misidentification  
- Your consent to the jurisdiction of the appropriate courts  
- Your physical or digital signature  

---

## 4. REPEAT OFFENDERS

NYX Studios reserves the right to permanently ban, block, or take legal
action against repeat offenders who persistently infringe copyrights.
